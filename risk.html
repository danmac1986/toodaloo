HOW IT WORKS

Risks

Arguably the biggest risk to this project’s success would be the user review and rating system. If the rating system doesn’t actually depict the real world, then the whole project would cause detrimental results. In order for it to be accurate, the formula used when calculating the results would have to consider variables such as users creating fake accounts or intentionally leaving fake reviews and inducing falsified data, resulting in inaccurate results. Alternatively another problem that usually happens with a lot of ratings is where someone tries to balance the rating with what they think it should be rather than what it actually is. An example of this would be someone giving a cleanliness rating of 1/10 even though it was more of a 4/10, only because the average review was higher than what they thought it should be. One way for us to avoid this error and to increase the likelihood of the results being real is to give more weight to those whose reviews are from a verified account, or they provided proof.
